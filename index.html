5-Day Gym Training Plan

Daily Warm-Up & Stretch (Protect Knees & Ligaments)

🔥 Warm-Up (5–8 Minutes)
  • Treadmill Walk / Cycling – 5 min
  • Leg Swings Front & Side – 15 each
  • Hip Circles – 15 reps
  • Knee Circles – 15 reps
  • Bodyweight Squats – 15 reps
🍑 Glute Activation
  • Glute Bridge – 2 x 15
  • Bodyweight Hip Thrust – 2 x 12
  • Step-ups – 2 x 10 each leg
🦵 Hamstrings Activation
  • Standing Hamstring Stretch – 20 sec each
  • Romanian Deadlift (Light) – 2 x 12
  • Heel Walks – 20 steps
🦵 Thigh (Quad) Activation
  • Wall Sit – 30 sec
  • Leg Extension (Light Weight) – 2 x 15
  • Walking Lunges – 10 each leg
🧘 Post Workout Stretch (Daily)
  • Quad Stretch – 30 sec each
  • Hamstring Stretch – 30 sec each
  • Calf Stretch – 30 sec each
  • Hip Flexor Stretch – 30 sec each


Day 1 — Legs

  • Squats (4) – 8–10
  • Leg Press (3) – 10–12
  • Leg Extension (3) – 12–15
  • Leg Curl (3) – 12–15
  • Standing Calf Raise (4) – 15–20
  • Walking Lunges (3) – 12 each leg


Day 2 — Chest + Triceps

Chest
  • Barbell Bench Press (4) – 8–10
  • Incline Dumbbell Press (3) – 10
  • Chest Fly Machine (3) – 12–15
  • Cable Crossover (3) – 12–15
Triceps
  • Tricep Pushdown (3) – 12
  • Overhead Dumbbell Extension (3) – 10–12
  • Assisted Dips (3) – 8–10
  • Close Grip Bench Press (3) – 8–10


Day 3 — Back + Biceps

Back
  • Lat Pulldown (4) – 10–12
  • Seated Cable Row (3) – 10–12
  • One-Arm Dumbbell Row (3) – 10 each side
  • Deadlift (3) – 5–8
Biceps
  • Barbell Curl (3) – 8–10
  • Dumbbell Alternate Curl (3) – 10–12
  • Hammer Curl (3) – 10–12
  • Preacher/Cable Curl (3) – 12


Day 4 — Shoulders + Core

Shoulders
  • Shoulder Press (4) – 8–10
  • Lateral Raise (4) – 12–15
  • Front Raise (3) – 12
  • Rear Delt Fly / Face Pull (3) – 12–15
Core
  • Plank (3) – 30–60 sec
  • Hanging Leg Raise (3) – 10–12
  • Russian Twist (3) – 20 reps
  • Crunch (3) – 10


Day 5 — Full Body + Conditioning

  • Pushups (3) – Max reps
  • Pullups / Assisted Pullups (3) – Max reps
  • Goblet Squats (3) – 12
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press (3) – 10–12
  • Cardio (10–15 min)
    • 1 min fast walk/jog
    • 1 min slow walk

Stay Consistent • Train Smart • Grow Daily
